Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use KikiQuiz, we may collect:

  • Account information: username, email address
  • Device information: country, preferred language
  • Game data: scores, levels reached, rankings
  • Identifiers: Firebase UID for authentication

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save your game progress
  • Display rankings and leaderboards
  • Improve the gaming experience
  • Communicate with you when necessary

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share information with:

  • Firebase (Google): for authentication and storage
  • Analytics services: anonymous data to improve the app

4. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure authentication with Firebase
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure credential storage

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Contact us with any questions

6. Children

KikiQuiz is intended for users of all ages. We do not intentionally collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@kikiquiz.com
