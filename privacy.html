Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use KikiQuiz, we may collect:

  • Account information: username, email address
  • Device information: country, preferred language, device model, operating system
  • Game data: scores, levels reached, rankings, inventory
  • Identifiers: Firebase UID for authentication, advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID), push notification tokens
  • Purchase data: transaction history for in-app purchases (processed by Apple/Google)
  • Crash and performance data: error logs, app performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Save your game progress
  • Display rankings and leaderboards
  • Improve the gaming experience
  • Communicate with you when necessary

3. Advertising

KikiQuiz displays ads to support the free version of the app. Our advertising partners may collect and use data to show you personalized ads:

  • Google AdMob: displays ads and may collect device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location. Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta Audience Network: displays ads and may collect similar data for ad personalization. Meta Privacy Policy

Premium subscribers do not see ads.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share information with:

  • Firebase (Google): for authentication, crash reporting (Crashlytics), and push notifications
  • Advertising partners: AdMob and Meta Audience Network for displaying ads
  • App stores: Apple App Store and Google Play for processing purchases
  • Analytics services: anonymous data to improve the app

5. In-App Purchases

KikiQuiz offers optional in-app purchases and subscriptions:

  • Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store)
  • We do not store your payment information (credit card, etc.)
  • We receive confirmation of purchases to deliver your items
  • Purchase history is stored to restore your purchases if needed

6. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure authentication with Firebase
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure credential storage

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings
  • Contact us with any questions

8. Children

KikiQuiz is intended for users of all ages. We do not intentionally collect information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are under 13, please use the app with parental supervision.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@kikiquiz.com
